Word Frenzy
Welcome to Word Frenzy, a game where you can compete against friends in a word guessing game
to see who can get the highest score.
  1. Create an account to play and keep track of you scores.
  2. The game has you trying to guess words in certain categories, and the more you get right the more points you score.
  3. Once logged in, you can play a game, create and edit categories of words,
    check your profile, check the leadeboard, and more.
